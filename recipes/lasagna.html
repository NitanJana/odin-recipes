Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and savory fillings, then baking it to perfection. It's a comforting and hearty culinary delight enjoyed by many around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles or pasta sheets
  • Ground meat (such as beef, pork, or a combination)
  • Onion and garlic (for flavor)
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Eggs (to bind the ricotta cheese)
  • Fresh or dried herbs (such as basil, oregano, or parsley)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil (for cooking)
  • Optional: vegetables (such as spinach or zucchini) or additional fillings (like mushrooms or sausage)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven. Boil and cook lasagna noodles. Drain.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil. Add ground meat, cook until browned. Drain fat.
  3. Add tomato sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and beaten eggs in a bowl.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until golden.
  8. Rest for a few minutes. Serve hot, garnished with cheese and herbs.
