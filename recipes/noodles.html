Noodles

Description

Long strands of wheat or rice, twirled in flavorful broth or sauce, creating a comforting and satisfying culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sauce/broth
  • Vegetables
  • Protein
  • Oil
  • Garlic/ginger
  • Soy sauce/seasonings
  • Garnishes

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Place the noodles into the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions or until desired doneness is achieved.
  4. Drain the cooked noodles and set them aside.
  5. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  6. Add garlic and/or ginger to the heated oil and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  7. Add vegetables and protein to the pan and stir-fry until cooked.
  8. Add sauce or broth to the pan and mix well.
  9. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together, ensuring the noodles are coated with the sauce.
  10. Cook for an additional minute or until the noodles are heated through.
  11. Remove from heat and garnish with desired toppings such as green onions, cilantro, or sesame seeds.
  12. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious noodles!
