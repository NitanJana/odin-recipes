Burger

Description

A burger is a delicious handheld creation, marrying juicy meat, cheese, and various toppings between soft, toasted bunsâ€”a timeless indulgence loved by food enthusiasts worldwide. Its simplicity belies its versatility, offering endless flavor combinations and satisfying cravings with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt & pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo)
  • Optional additions (bacon, mushrooms, avocado, fried egg)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Season and shape ground beef into patties.
  3. Cook patties for 4-5 minutes on the first side, then flip.
  4. Add cheese during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast burger buns lightly.
  6. Remove patties from heat and let them rest.
  7. Place patties on bottom halves of buns.
  8. Add toppings.
  9. Cover with top halves of buns.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
